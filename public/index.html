Our Story

It all began when co-founder, Ashok Mathur traveled through South America during a hiatus from his current position at a large, well-known, EHR company. (Patagonia was one of his favorite places – hence our name!) He thought “there has to be a better way.” People want to be treated as people, and deserve to get a quality product that is backed by a high level of product support. They deserve a holistic approach to training and implementation and a vendor that is accessible to them at all times.

So, in 2009, upon return from his travels, Ashok approached Abhi Muthiyan with his ideas. With the technical know-how, Abhi worked through the software development process, while Ashok managed the daily business start-up and Patagonia Health became a reality. Patagonia Health was founded with a focus on solving two major user problems: to provide a modern, easy-to-use EHR software, and just as importantly, to provide excellent customer service.

Today we remain true to those founding principles with a goal of 100% customer reference ability, because more than buying software, customers trust us to hold their hand through their transition to electronic health records. We support and recommend industry best practices and share our expertise to help our customers improve. We are truly a resource for them in every way.
